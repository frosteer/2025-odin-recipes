Mushroom Soup

Description:

Tasty soup my waufu likes to cook.

Ingredients:

  1. Mushroom
  2. Water
  3. Chicken cube
  4. Salt & pepper
  5. Worcestershire sauce

Steps:

  • Cut the mushroom.
  • Boil the water.
  • Throw everything inside.
  • Stir until fully cooked.
  • Ready to be served
