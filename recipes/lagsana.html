Lagsana

Description:

Lasagna is an Italian baked pasta dish made of flat pasta sheets layered with meat sauce (usually beef or pork in tomato sauce), creamy béchamel or ricotta cheese, and melted mozzarella. It’s baked until golden and bubbly — rich, hearty, and full of flavor.

Ingredients:

  1. Lasagna noodles – flat pasta sheets that form the layers.
  2. Ground meat – usually beef or a beef–pork mix for the sauce.
  3. Tomato sauce – gives the lasagna its rich flavor.
  4. Cheese – typically mozzarella, ricotta, and parmesan.
  5. Onion and garlic – for the base flavor of the sauce.

Steps:

  • Cook the meat sauce – sauté onion and garlic, add ground meat, tomato sauce, herbs, salt, and pepper.
  • Prepare the cheese mixture – mix ricotta (or cottage cheese) with egg, mozzarella, and parmesan.
  • Boil the lasagna noodles – cook until slightly soft, then drain.
  • Layer the lasagna – alternate noodles, meat sauce, and cheese mixture in a baking dish.
  • Bake – bake in the oven (about 180°C / 350°F) for 30–40 minutes until golden and bubbly.
