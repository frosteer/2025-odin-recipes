Pizza

Description:

Pizza is my favourite.

Ingredients:

  1. Flour
  2. Cheese
  3. Tomato paste
  4. Mushroom
  5. Chicken

Steps:

  • Make the dough.
  • Cut chicken and mushroom to tiny pieces.
  • Put tomato paste on the kneaded dough.
  • Put the chicken and mushroom on the kneaded dough and bake in ohot oven 50' degree for 15 minutes.
  • Ready to be served.
